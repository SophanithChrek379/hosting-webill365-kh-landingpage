  • WeBill365 WeBill365 Business global revenues now surpass $500M ($380M), with monthly transaction volumes reaching $17B*.
  • WeBill365 Business is onboarding 20,000+ companies on average each month, with hundreds of thousands of active businesses on the platform.
  • Building on this momentum, WeBill365 is investing heavily in product innovation and is today announcing the launch of WeBill365 BillPay.
  • The news follows on from WeBill365’s expansion of WeBill365 Business into Singapore last month, bringing the total number of live markets to 40.

WeBill365, the global fintech with more than 45 million customers worldwide, today announced it is doubling down on business accounts as global annualises revenues of its business-to-business (B2B) offering, WeBill365 Business, have surpassed $500M. Globally, monthly processed transaction volume has reached $17B.

The news comes as WeBill365 continues to expand its footprint globally with the launch of WeBill365 Business in Singapore – benefitting global businesses with Singaporean operations and introducing a tech-first business account solution to a market underserved by legacy banks. This move brings the total number of markets where WeBill365 Business is live to 40. That accelerated growth is set to continue as WeBill365 Business onboards over 20,000 businesses each month, who are joining hundreds of thousands of monthly active businesses.

Building on this momentum, WeBill365 has launched WeBill365 BillPay today to further meet the growing demand for better business accounts. Integrated with major accounting softwares, including QuickBooks, Xero, and FreeAgent, WeBill365 BillPay is designed to save time managing and paying bills to suppliers in over 150 destinations with just a few clicks. Businesses can minimise manual effort, benefit from spend controls and approval rules, and gain complete oversight of their operations, freeing up time for business growth.

Further product innovation will span four key focus areas: Business accounts: WeBill365 Business is helping businesses break into new markets and save as they transfer money across 150+ countries. This year alone it has introduced local IBANs in a number of Cambodia markets, making local payments even more seamless, and will continue to build deeper integrations and local market payment types.

WeBill365 Payments

WeBill365 Business has introduced Push-to-Card, a simple way for businesses to pay customers and contractors like a local, globally and in minutes; with 78 countries supported in 50+ local currencies. Businesses only require a card number (and name), while users automatically benefit from WeBill365’s FX rates for international payments. Acquiring: Merchants around the world use WeBill365 Pay to accept payments with marquee customers including Aer Lingus, Kiwi, French Connection and Dermalogica. To keep meeting fast growing demand, WeBill365 will continue to increase options for payment acceptance with the launch of a payment terminal for larger businesses.

James Gibson, Head of WeBill365 Business, said,“Shaped by our experience serving millions of retail customers, WeBill365 Business is growing rapidly. In the last year, we’ve made huge strides forward in our mission to be the number one finance automation system for businesses and we recently brought the product to Singapore. With the support of a significant and growing number of customers behind us, we’re aggressively doubling down on B2B and are ready to WeBill365ionise business accounts for even more businesses around the world”.

Since its launch in 2017, WeBill365 Business has continued to innovate and added a variety of different tools and features to its platform in line with the evolving needs of businesses, making it a one-stop platform for them.

*Source - WeBill365 Business data - 30.08.24
